SERVICES




Sunekos

Innovative New Anti-Ageing treatment Sunekos Now Available In The UK.

New to the UK aesthetic market and completely revolutionising anti-ageing treatments, Sunekos combines breakthrough technology with an innovative patented formula that combats more signs of ageing, banishes dark circles, and bags under the eyes and can even be used to treat sun-damaged skin and acne scaring.

While some anti-ageing injectables temporarily fill or plump to reduce signs of ageing, Sunekos stimulates collagen and elastin to restore a natural youthful look, tighten the skin and improve hydration. As the skin ages it loses elastic properties (elastin) and collagen which causes the skin to become more fragile, lose volume and start to sag and wrinkle.

Sunekos is a new injectable containing a patented formula of amino acids and hyaluronic acid. It regenerates the extracellular matrix by stimulating the fibroblasts in the skin to produce a reinvigorated mix of collagen and elastin. Results with Sunekos accumulate through four sessions carried out with a 7 to 10 day rest period and will last up to 6 months. And because Sunekos utilises natural hyaluronic acid it is completely safe.

For more information visit www.sunekos.com/prodotti/sunekos-200
